I'm working towards being a coder.

I'm a TV/Computer technician. I've been using HTML and CSS for since around 2000 when I designed a website to showcase an exhibition of horror video covers and posters that I had organised in our library. I took night classes in Visual Basic, Dreamweaver and Flash, before I discovered online courses. These courses helped me to maintain a website at my workplace, and also a couple of sites that I designed for local roofing businesses. More recently, I have been trying to expand my knowledge of coding with Python, JavaScript and PHP at Code Academy, Coursera and Free Code Camp.

I was feeling overwhelmed by a multitude of online tutorials and eBooks, and was beginning to despair as I wasn't sure how to progress - or even if I was learning the right things.
